About Museum of Illusion Kuala Lumpur

The Museum of Illusions is a 21st-century museum that offers an interactive and unique experience designed to trick the mind, challenge your vision, and make you question all your senses.All exhibits are based on science, mathematics, psychology, and art—allowing you to learn a great deal about vision, perception, and the human brain in a fun and interactive way!

Vision& Mission

Vision

  • To be Malaysia’s leading centre for illustration art—celebrating and promoting the creative power of visual storytelling, fostering innovation, and elevating Kuala Lumpur as a vibrant hub for illustrators, enthusiasts, and the wider public.

Mission

  • Collect, conserve, and exhibit original and contemporary illustration artworks—ranging from Malaysian traditions to global movements.
