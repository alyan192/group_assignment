Our Exhibits

Explore a collection of rooms that can shift your perspective, challenge your mind, and captivate you with fascinating visuals. Our exhibits reveal that things are not always as they seem—especially at the Museum of Illusions. Get ready to be amazed!

The Tilted Room will surely put your mind to the test and make you question the laws of physics! Lean forward or bend backward – you won’t believe what you’re seeing!

The Vortex Tunnel will trick your brain into thinking you’ve lost your balance! Your mind sends signals that the ground beneath you is moving, even though you’re actually walking on a flat, stable surface. Come give it a try!

Are you ready to experience a shift in perspective? You might just find what you’re looking for — the moment you step into our Upside Down Room!

Didn’t think cloning was possible? Well, it is—at least here! Discover one of our most popular exhibits.

Try our famous Beuchet Chair, where anything seems possible — even children can appear taller than adults!

Discover one of our classic illusions that’s perfect for a hilarious photo !

Dive into our amazing collection of 3D images that suddenly disappear and reappear, change their motif, or pop out of their frame, and learn about the fascinating technology of holograms.

Want to learn more about the amazing human brain and how it understands perspective? Nothing demonstrates it better than our illusion images — the perfect proof!

Our incredible set of optical illusions will play with your perception and reveal the mysteries of the human mind.
